Jack Rowley

He made 424 appearances for Manchester United and was the first of only four players to score 200 goals for the club.

Tommy Taylor

He scored 131 goals in just 161 appearances for Manchester United. He was one of eight players to be killed in the Munich air disaster.

Bobby Charlton

He is United's second-highest goalscorer and has the second-most appearances for the club. He captained the club from 1968 to 1973.

George Best

He made 470 appearances for United, and is considered one of the greatest footballers of all time.

Paul Scholes

He made the third-highest number of appearances for Manchester United, with 718.
